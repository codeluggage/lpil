Our pricing model is totally transparent and competitive.

It is based on a price ‘per room’ per quarter model, not ‘per user’*. Once we have had a fair value of usage conversation and agree pricing based on your requirements, the quarterly fees will be fully inclusive of:

  • Full training and implementation
  • Unlimited users
  • Unlimited usage
  • Ongoing 24/7 support
  • All future upgrades and enhancements

*In our opinion, the price ‘per user’ model for a board portal is fundamentally flawed, as the number of users first envisaged invariably grows out of control creating unforeseen additional costs.
