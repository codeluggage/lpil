The VBR App is now available on the App Store helping directors to stay connected wherever they are. It is director specific, meaning that if a director sits on more than one board using Virtualboardroom, they will only need one point of entry.

Key features of the app include:

  • Secure authentication
  • Instantly updated board packs and shared documents
  • App PIN lock
  • Document annotation including text notes, strikeout, highlight and freehand
  • Document bookmarks for simple navigation
  • Offline document viewing
  • Document search
  • Note summary page created from your annotations

To locate our app, go to the App Store and search for ‘Virtualboardroom’, or click here.
