This case study refers to one of our clients who implemented Virtualboardroom.

The Challenge

It took the company secretary two days per month to put together the printed board packs for each of the ten board members. The process was costing the company Â£4,310 per year. They were determined to find the most cost effective solution whilst increasing their efficiency and improving their environmental impact.

The Solution

The company secretary implemented Virtualboardroom in order to make it easier for board members to collaborate over documentation, reduce the time it takes to produce board packs, and facilitate digital distribution of the packs to board members.

Choosing iPads

The company bought each of the board members a standard 3G/16GB iPad after assessing the relative savings of using iPads with Virtualboardroom, compared to printing hard copies. Investing in Virtualboardroom and an iPad for each board member has reduced overall board pack production expenditure by 44%.

The Figures

  • 1,800 employees
  • 10 board members
  • 250 pages on average
  • 12 meetings a year
  • 5 working weeks saved per year
  • 44% cost reduction
  • 12 iPads purchased

Savings thanks to Virtualboardroom

Virtualboardroom has saved the company secretary nearly five working weeks per year, allowing them time to get on with more business critical tasks. Board members also report feeling more up to date, having access to their documents from wherever they are in the world.
