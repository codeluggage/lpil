Your Guide to the Investor Magazine

The Investor is a coffee-table style magazine that endorses our professional and expert approach. We strongly recommended that Partners personalise copies so their contact details and photo are contained within each issue. Once you have placed an order this will keep rolling for all future editions unless you place an amendment or cancellation to order during one of the relevant windows below.

Distribution Schedule

The Investor Magazine is published quarterly:


Edition Ordering/Amendment Window Delivery Schedule
Spring Edition February* Last week in April
Summer Edition May* Last week in July
Autumn Edition August* Last week in October
Winter Edition November* Last week in January

* lasts approximately 4 weeks in the month shown


A covering letter will be available on the Intranet just before each edition is distributed.
