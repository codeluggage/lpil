The Investor

  • Can you tell me my current Investor order?

    • Ypu can check your current Investor order by clicking the Investor tab and click the link "Review Your Previous Investor And Investment Review Order Here".This will give you a breakdown of your order and also provide the latest PDF Perivan hold for your order.

  • When can I amend the quantity on my Investor order?

    • The Investor is open for a period of time each quarter for you to amend the quantity amongst other details. The general opening time is 4-6 weeks and in this period you must make the amendment. After this time Perivan will not be able to accept any changes to your order.

  • How do I amend my photo/signature?

    • If you require a change of photo and signature you can submit your photo via the Investor tab above. You must make sure the photo is of a good size and resolution. Perivan will inform you if the photo is unsuitable. Once you submit the photo and signature it will send an email to Perivan who will upload them to your Partner profile. Please do not place any orders until you recieve a conformation email from Perivan confirming action has been taken.

  • How do I amend my personalisation?

    • If your personal data has changed then you will need to update your profile on the store located in the “My Profile” tab. Once you have made the changes then you will need to click the link located in the menu titled “Investor” and click the subcategory "Personalised". The option you need to pick is “Amend Personalisation to an Existing Order”.If your unsure how to action this, we have provided a video and a guide located under the investor tab to help you.

  • My Investor hasn't been delivered to my address and the profile shows the correct address?

    • Whenever your data changes on the store by either yourself or a office administrator and this affects your Investor order, please be aware you must always place a amended order. This applies to your delivery address and personalised details as this doesn’t update the existing order until you do this. If you do not make any changes to your order then it will continue to roll as per last editions. Please refer to the Investor Guide as this goes into greater detail.

  • Delivery Schedules

    • Stocked Items - Out of Office Partners: i.e Marketing Literature, Folders, Envelopes, Continuation Paper etc.

    We aim to deliver all orders placed for “Out of Office” Partners before 2pm Monday to Friday on the next working day. If your order hasn’t arrived after the time stated then please contact Perivan.

    Stocked Items – In Office Partners: i.e. Marketing Literature, Folders, Envelopes, Continuation Paper etc.

    We aim to deliver all orders placed for “Out of Office” Partners before 12pm Monday to Friday on the next working day. Please note “In Office” deliveries are often consolidated to minimize delivery costs. Your office location may receive boxes that contain orders for more than one partner, please check all deliveries carefully. If your order hasn’t arrived after the time stated then please contact Perivan.

  • Print on Demand Literature

    • We aim to deliver all orders for Print on Demand literature 4-5 working days from the date of your order. If your order hasn’t arrived after the time stated then please contact Perivan.

  • SJP Business Stationery

    • i.e. Business Cards, Compliment Slips and Letterheads We aim to deliver all orders for standard St. James’s Place branded business stationery 5-7 working days from the date it is approved via the online approvals system. If your order hasn’t arrived after the time stated then please contact Perivan.

  • Partner Branded Stationery

    • i.e. Business Cards, Compliment Slips, Letterheads and Continuation Paper which have been personalized with your own bespoke logo or layout. We aim to deliver all orders for bespoke business stationery 10-12 working days from the date it is approved. If your order hasn’t arrived after the time stated then please contact Perivan.

  • How to cancel your order?

    • Stationary i.e.Business Cards, Compliment Slips and Letterheads

    If you have approved incorrect stationery then please contact Perivan immediately. Depending on what stage your items have been processed you could incur administrative charges for cancelling or the full the cost of the stationery. So please take care in approving your order.

    Literature i.e. Marketing Literature, Folders, Envelopes, Continuation Paper etc.

    If you need to cancel your literature orders then please email your order reference number to Perivan. Also we would advise you call to highlight your email to the Perivan team as during high periods of enquires they may not reach your email in time to halt the delivery. Be advised the couriers arrive between 3.30pm and 4pm to dispatch Partners orders.
