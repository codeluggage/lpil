What it is

Virtualboardroom®

is a board portal which allows you, very securely, to collaborate with others in the compilation of the board pack and then to share that and other key board information with directors. The directors can view their documents in hard copy printed format or in electronic format on any device or through our dedicated VBR App for iPad viewing and annotation.

Boardpack®

is a tool that sits within the portal which, very quickly, takes all your report files – Word, Excel, PowerPoint and PDF – and brings them together into one consolidated board pack.

It is being used by company secretaries, administrators and boards the world over and can be accessed from anywhere at anytime.

Who it’s for

Company secretaries and administrators

Collaborate with key contributors to board packs, streamline your production process and make information available to directors on the move.

Directors, board members and trustees

All your board or committee information in one neat, secure area:

  • Current board packs
  • Archived board packs
  • Shared documents containing corporate governance information
  • Calendar
  • Search facility
