We demonstrate the product by either visiting you in person or via the web and give you indicative pricing based on your requirements.
If you like what you see and hear, we offer a free trial period, ideally covering a board meeting cycle, to test the software and our support.
If you still like it, we meet to discuss the trial experience, answer any questions, agree pricing and exchange contracts.
We set up your rooms, train your administration team and directors if required and away you go!
