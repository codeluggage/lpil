DotApprove is an innovative collaboration, approval and content sharing software service.

It brings marketing and compliance teams together to project manage, develop and sign-off all documents and marketing materials (in any format) quickly and efficiently.

DotApprove will streamline and enforce your brand and compliance approval, reduce the risk of failing to meet industry standards, speed up the creation of collateral, and better connect your teams.

DotApprove is easily customised, so regardless of the size and structure of your organisation, we can work together to create the perfect software system for you.
