The Company

W.R. Berkley Syndicate Management Limited

The Sector

Lloyd’s Insurance

The Project

PRA document request

The Challenge

Compile 106 separate files totalling 1,417 pages, and print just 2 copies

Once they had received all the files, the Perivan team compiled them into one consolidated PDF with sequential page numbering and index tabs throughout. Even though the documents were sent piecemeal and late in the day, the Perivan team worked with me, staying into the evening to ensure my document was printed, bound and delivered on time.

Having a direct delivery service to my client only added to a seamless and holistic service, one I could not have duplicated in-house without considerable time and effort.

Working with Perivan felt like working with an extension of the WRB team based on their ‘can do’ attitude and their pro-active nature to ensure WRB’s professional brand was maintained in terms of document output and deadline delivery.

Debbie Cudbill
Corporate Governance
